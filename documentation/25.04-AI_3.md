# NeuralSonic - 24.04


## What I did
I decided to make the AI smarter, to study from the user, the real player based on their behaviour, their inputs. How does this work? Well when the player starts playing their movements are recorded until they die, then they're recorded again and again and... (until they switch to AI mode ofc). The data is saved in a .json file and when the user switches to AI mode the AI gathers this data, the steps, the movements from the user. This way it's more trained and it can make more progress. 

### Problems in this part:
- the delay when the user switches between modes

There was this delay, because the script had to gather the data from the json file when the player movements were recorded and to put it in the neural network for training the AI.
